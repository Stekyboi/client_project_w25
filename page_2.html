Welcome to Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

You Matter!

CAPS is committed to creating an environment based on our values of multicultural, multi-disciplinary and multi-theoretical practices that allow our diverse student body to access care, receive high quality services and take positive pathways to mental health. We are happy to offer both in-person and virtual services.

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) is here to support your mental health and well-being throughout your college journey. We offer confidential, compassionate care to help you navigate challenges like stress, anxiety, depression, relationship issues, and more. Our licensed counselors, psychologists, and social workers specialize in working with college students and understand the unique pressures of university life. Whether you're seeking short-term support or need help connecting to long-term resources, CAPS is here for you.

Our Front Desk (Michigan Union 4th Floor) is open and ready to help support you -- staff members are available throughout the day to assist you with your needs to answer questions, and get you to the right place. We can be reached by phone at: (734) 764-8312.

Services We Provide

At CAPS, we offer a variety of mental health services tailored to meet your needs:

  • Individual Counseling: Work one-on-one with a counselor to explore and address personal challenges.
  • Group Therapy: Join supportive spaces with peers to share experiences and learn new coping skills.
  • Crisis Intervention: Get immediate support for urgent mental health concerns. Our crisis counselors are available during regular hours, with after-hours support for emergencies.
  • Workshops and Outreach: Attend educational workshops on stress management, mindfulness, and resilience.
  • Psychiatric Services: Access medication evaluations and management with our licensed psychiatrists.
  • Referrals: If you need ongoing or specialized care, we can connect you with trusted providers in the community.
